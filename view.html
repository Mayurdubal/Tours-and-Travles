Tourist Destinations

Explore our popular tourist destinations

Mahabaleshwar

1.Wilson Point: Also known as Sunrise Point, Wilson Point offers breathtaking panoramic views of the sunrise and surrounding valleys. It is the highest point in Mahabaleshwar. 2.Venna Lake: A serene and scenic lake surrounded by lush greenery, offering boating, horse riding, and leisurely walks along the promenade. 3.Arthur's Seat: A popular viewpoint offering spectacular views of the Savitri River, lush valleys, and deep gorges. It is known for its "Queen of the Points" status. 4.Pratapgarh Fort: Located near Mahabaleshwar, this historic fort offers stunning views of the surrounding landscape and is known for its historical significance.

Cost: 10000

Duration: 5 days

Miami

1.South Beach: Known for its stunning sandy shores, colorful lifeguard stands, and vibrant Art Deco architecture along Ocean Drive. 2.Art Deco Historic District: Explore the world's largest collection of Art Deco architecture in Miami's historic district, featuring pastel-colored buildings, boutique hotels, and trendy cafes. 3.Wynwood Walls: A must-visit for art enthusiasts, Wynwood Walls is an outdoor street art gallery showcasing vibrant murals by renowned artists from around the world.

Cost: 690000

Duration: 5 days

Japan

Japan is a popular tourist destination, attracting millions of visitors each year. Tourists are drawn to its vibrant cities, historic temples and shrines, beautiful natural landscapes, hot springs (onsen), and unique cultural experiences such as sumo wrestling and traditional Japanese theater (kabuki and noh).

Cost: 90000

Duration: 5 days

Dubai

1.Burj Khalifa: The tallest building in the world, offering panoramic views of the city from its observation decks. 2.Palm Jumeirah: An iconic man-made island known for its luxury resorts, beaches, and Atlantis, The Palm resort. 3.Dubai Mall: One of the largest shopping malls globally, featuring over 1,200 retail outlets, an indoor ice rink, an aquarium, and a cinema complex. 4.Burj Al Arab: A luxury hotel often referred to as the "sail-shaped" building, renowned for its opulence and iconic design.

Cost: 490000

Duration: 5 days
